Review

Travis Steele 4/26/2026

Hot Fuzz is a great cop movie in my opinion becasue it does a great job of putting all of the stereotypical tropes of all cop movies into one movie. I love the action that took place in the second half of the film, as well with the forshadowing in the first half of the movie. I think that Simon Pegg and Nick Frost have a great comedic dynamic in this movie.

My favorite scenes have to be the ones with the sea mine that they confiscated from an old farmer and the shootouts. The unexpected twists that take place throughout the film are well timed and kept me guessing.


Finally, this movie is part of one of the best trilogies in my opinion.

Trivia

  • Did you know the "greater good" running joke was improvised and almost cut from the film.
  • Almost every major British actor they approached agreed to appear, which is why the film is packed with recognizable faces like Timothy Dalton, Jim Broadbent, and Bill Nighy.
  • The infamous swan that Nicholas Angel chases was a real swan and was completely uncooperative on set. Wrangling it took way longer than anyone expected.
  • The village residents murdering people over a flower show competition was apparently not considered that far-fetched by people who have actually been to English village flower shows.
